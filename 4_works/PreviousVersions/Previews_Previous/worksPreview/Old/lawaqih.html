Description
A record of the discussions on various topics which Ibn Sawdakīn (d. 646) had with his master Ibn ʿArabī. The content includes that of K. al-Wasāʾil by Ibn Sawdakīn (RG 436), which is really an extract of this longer work.

Manuscripts
مخطوطات
OY states that there are no extant mss of this work, but three copies have now been found: the earliest is Zahiriyya 16632, which is in the hand of al-Farghānī, probably dateable to 660s. There are also much later less reliable copies in the Budeiri library, 142, and in the Dar al-Kutub, Cairo, Taymur 147. Further mss identified by JC: Damascus al-Assad (http://www.alassad-library.gov.sy/Search_makhtotat2013.php): 16627/2 which follows IA’s ʿAqīda, dated 955.
