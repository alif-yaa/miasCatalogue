Description
الوصف
Treatise on four kinds of knowers (ʿulamāʾ) at the Well of Arīn (axis mundi): those of the west (maghrib), those of the east (mashriq), those of the north (shimāl) and those of the south (yamīn), and their kinds of knowledge. See also FM 1:38-41.
Manuscripts
مخطوطات
OY cites seven mss. Verified by one historic ms.,Veliyuddin 51 (762H, copied from an original composed in Damascus in 602H). Milli 2988 is the same text, but the other two mss. we have consulted are different texts. Confused with RG 34 and others. Further work needed. Further mss identified by JC: Tunis, Dar al-Kutub al-Wataniya 641, and Escurial ms 417 / 4 (f. 117-131?)
