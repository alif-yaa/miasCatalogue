بديل
English
إنجليزي
Ibn al-ʿArabī observes the following (Marconi 2023, 106):

وغَرْضي في هذه العجالة أن نبيّن مقام الشَيْخوخة ولوازمها ومقام المريد ولوازمه وما ينبغي أن يتعامل به أهلُ طريق الله ويعاملوا به طريق الله تعالى. ولهذا سمّيتها: "الأمر المُحْكَم المربوط فيما يلزم أهلَ طريق الله تعالى من الشروط". فإن الزمان مشحون بالدعاوى الكاذبة العريضة، فلا مريد صادق ثابت القدم في سلوكه، ولا شيخ محقّق ينصحه ويخرجه من رعونته نفسه وإعجابه برأيه ويعرّب له عن طريق الحق؛ فالمريد يدّعي الشَيْخوخة والرئاسة، وهذا كله تخليط وتلبيس.

Description
The title translates as “The Firm Rule regarding the conditions necessary for people on God’s Path”. The aim of the work is to give advice to spiritual guides on the kind of language and teaching which should be offered to different types of students.

Manuscripts
مخطوطات
A much copied work, verified by at least six historic mss, three of them definitely copied from originals. The best are Shehit Ali 1341, written around 724H in Damascus, copied from an original by IA and certified by him, and Ayasofya 4875 (probably dated 705H). OY believed the work was written in Konya in 602H on basis of a note in Veliyuddin 51 (110b), but further inspection of the note shows that the place of writing is Damascus. (See Jane Clark and Denis McAuley, Some Notes on the Manuscript Veliyuddin 51, JMIAS, 40 (2006), pp. 101-116.)

Clark and McAuley, 2006: At the end, on 110b, there is a note saying that the book was completed in Damascus in Jumâdâ al-ûlâ 602 by the hand of its author […] Osman Yahia asserts that the text was completed in Konya in 602, based (he says) on evidence within the text.[21] We can only assume that this refers to three lines from the bottom on 95b: wa-laqad haddathanî Awhad al-dîn Hâmid ibn Abî l-Fakhr al-Kirmânî bi-manzilî fî madînat Qûniya fî shahr Safar sanat 602 (“Awhad al-dîn Kirmânî related to me in my home in the city of Konya in Safar 602 …”).
