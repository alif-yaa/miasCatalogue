Description
الوصف
An early work, written in the Maghreb, but we do not know whether the version we have is the same as the first recension. It consists of 14 contemplations in the style of Niffarī’s Mawāqif. There are two commentaries, one by Ibn Sawdakīn and one by Sitt al-ʿAjam, which have been used in a critical Spanish edition and an English translation of Mashāhid. There are two associated works; a prologue which is classifed by OY as RG 626, and a commentary by Ibn Sawdakīn classified as RG 684.
Manuscripts
مخطوطات
A much copied work, verified by three historic mss. The best is probably Manisa 1183 (651H, copied by a direct disciple of IA’s) and Veliyuddin 1782 (929H, copied from an original dated 596H). Some texts include the prologue (RG 626) and an epilogue. We have classified all these under RG 432 as it is too complex to make distinctions. Michel Chodkiewicz has examined both the Manisa ms, and the Paris National ms (B. N. 6104). He finds a slight difference between them in Ibn ʿArabi’s commentary.
