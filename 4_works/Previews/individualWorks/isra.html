Description
الوصف
Description, in rhyming couplets, of IA’s miʿrāj (spiritual ascension) in Fez, written very soon after the event in 594H.
Manuscripts
مخطوطات
OY cites 30 mss., of which three can be considered historic. The best is Veliyuddin 1628 (read to the author in 633H), which formed the basis of Souad al-Hakim’s critical edition. Ragib Pasha 1453, whilst it is very late, is taken from SQ’s original.
Once the manuscript data has been migrated, it will be linked to the relevant works in the new system in a very similar way to the current website.
